Utility & Mechanics

$LDGR powers thepark.world — an autonomous markets desk with live edges across NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, commodities & stocks. A Solana immutable ledger bridging TradFi accounting integrity with DeFi: verifiable infrastructure for asset protection, global transactions, and transparent AI-agent labor tracking.

Autonomous Markets Access

Settlement and access token for the autonomous markets desk — live edges across major sports leagues, commodities and equities.

Asset Protection Ledger

Immutable on-chain accounting bridging TradFi integrity with DeFi transparency for protected, auditable balances.

AI-Agent Labor Tracking

Every autonomous agent action is recorded as a verifiable ledger entry — transparent attribution of machine labor.

Governance

Token-weighted voting over treasury deployment, desk parameters and protocol upgrades through transparent proposals.

Staking

Stake $LDGR to underwrite desk liquidity and earn a share of protocol revenue distributed from realized edges.

Fee Burn

A portion of desk settlement fees is permanently burned on-chain, deflationary against the fixed 1B supply.

Transparency

Programmatic Source of Truth

Circulating supply is calculated in real time via a plain-text API endpoint that queries the smart contract's totalSupply() and programmatically subtracts the balances of all locked / vesting wallet addresses. This ensures CoinMarketCap, CoinGecko and other aggregators always see verified, tamper-proof data.
